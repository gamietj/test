About Us

About Our Company

Zanoxolo Emphakatini Construction CC ('ZEC') is a start-up enterprise that is owned and managed by Fikile Mnisi ('Fikile') who has more than 15 years of experience in the construction industry.

Due to the vast amount of experience attained by the owner, ZEC is capable of handling a wide diversity of retail, industrial, commercial and housing projects. The owner's experience is further complimented by vast range of experience employees.

ZEC serves both private and public markets and deliver outstanding value with our depth of experience in both large and small projects.

We are a 100% black owned Exempt Micro-Enterprise with 40% of the ownership being in hands of black women. The entity has a Level 1 B-BBEE Rating with a 110% B-BBEE Procurement Recognition.

Strategy & Vision

Our business is all about teams and our success has been built on the strong teamwork we've forged on every one of our projects. We understand that each team member - the client, design professional and construction specialist - brings specific knowledge and strength to the project team. We stress an approach that builds on these strengths, enabling all members to work closely and efficiently as a team specifically assembled to deliver the project. The advantages of this approach stem from trust and cooperation between owner, architect and constructor.

The benefits are:

  • Complete control over all aspects of your project
  • Collaboration rather than confrontation
  • Architect and constructor each working in client's best interest
  • Effective delivery of high quality for a defined cost
  • Shortened overall project time
  • Real-life construction input during design work
  • Cost commitment early in design phase
  • Continuous review of construction documents

Our Vision

Our focus is to be a premier construction company by exceeding industry standards and customer expectations. We aim to execute our projects in such a way as to contribute towards capacity building in communities we operate. Employment creation and the transfer of knowledge and skills to local communities are part of our main objectives.

Our Mission

  • Earning our customers' enthusiasm through continuous improvement, professional competence and personal care
  • Integrity through maintaining the highest level of ethical conduct in every decision we make
  • Providing a platform that encourages dreams, relationships and opportunities
  • Individual respect and responsibility towards ourselves and the community we serve
  • To strengthen trust and honour in our trade

Our Values

Being "value-led" is the heart of ZEC's planned success. Some of the company's principal values are - putting client interests first, keeping commitments made, making sure everyone wins, doing whatever it takes, valuing and respecting others and doing it the safe way.

Our Growth Strategy

Due to the vast experience of our management team and staff our main growth strategy is true diversification. Our flexibility allows us to embark on different projects at the same time irrespective of the project size. Further growth strategies include special focus on branding to create customer awareness.
